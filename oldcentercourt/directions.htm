Directions

Center Court Tennis Shop is located inside the New Canaan Racquet Club, up the main stairs. Parking is available underneath the building.

From the Merritt Parkway

Coming from North or South, Take exit 37. Follow route 124 North for 2 miles toword New Canaan. At the end of the road turn left onto Elm Street. Follow Elm through one traffic light. Take the first left onto Grove Street. the New Canaan Racquet Club is on the right, just before the rail road tracks. Parking is underneath the building.

From CT I-95

Coming North or South take exit 15 (Route 7 North). Follow route 7 north to the Merritt Parkway South (15) Exit. Take the Merritt Parkway South to exit 37. Follow above directions.
