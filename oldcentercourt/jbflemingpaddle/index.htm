2010 Jane B. Fleming Memorial Tournament

  • Entry fee is $110/team. To pay by check, please make payable to Center Court Tennis, and mail to 45 Grove St. New Canaan, CT 06840. To pay by credit card, call Nick Bergman at (203) 253-7435.
  • Deadline is Tuesday, November 23, 2010.
  • Players must be current APTA members or pay a $20 Limited Membership Fee. Good for one tournament. Call the APTA Office at 888-744-9490 or apply online at platformtennnis.org to purchase a membership.
  • Entry fee covers balls, favors, snacks, lunch, and fun competition (priceless)!
  • Draw size is limited. Please sign up early. Draws will be posted here and the APTA web site.
  • Wednesday is main draw and early consolation rounds. Thursday will be semifinals and finals for all draws. Please plan accordingly.
  • Weather permitting, all entrants will be scheduled for three matches. All matches will be best 2 out of 3 sets. The APTA 12 point tiebreaker will be used throughout.
