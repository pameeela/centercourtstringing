Welcome to Center Court, a mobile stringing service as well as a full-service pro shop located at the New Canaan Racquet Club.

Center Court pro shop carries tennis apparel and equipment for every player, including a full line of platform tennis paddles and apparel.

We also offer expert stringing services. Racquet pick-up and delivery is available at your house for your convenience, at any location in Stamford, New Canaan, Darien, or Greenwich, for $1 each way. This fee will be waived if we can pick up and deliver two or more racquets at once in the same location. Your racquet will be expertly re-strung by a professional.
