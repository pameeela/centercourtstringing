Elite 3 Ball Machine Rental

The Lobster Elite Model 3 tennis ball machine is available for rent for the flat rate of $19 Monday-Friday or $24 Saturday and Sunday. Call or e-mail to make a reservation.

About the Elite Model 3

The Elite 3 offers triple oscillation and narrow and wide two-line features. You can warm up your forehand and backhand, challenge your footwork and reaction time and much, much more.

A first-class tennis ball machine, the Elite 3 truly simulates professional match play with two versatile pre-programmed options for narrow and wide forehand and backhand. While you're trying to maintain your dignity against the triple oscillation and the two-line drill, it will confront you with up to 50 degree lobs to practice your overheads - a feature not available on any other ball machine. With speeds up to 80 miles per hour, it will challenge your ability to react, and heavy top- and back-spin choices to perfect your ability to return whatever comes your way.
